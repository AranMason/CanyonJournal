The current name of the App is CanyonJournal

The goal of this ticket is to find a new name that is not similar to exisiting UK based canyoning servies and outlets such as:
- Canyon Log
- Nae Limits
- V7 Academy
- Canyoning Company
- Galloway Canyoning
- Canyon Zone
- Adventure Activities Wales

Ideally we don't share the first few letters with other major services so we can be easily auto completed. 

Our other goal is well, is to indicate to people our purpose
- WE ARE a journalling app, targetted at people to record thier adventures
- WE ARE NOT a place to find new canyons, beta, or topo for a canyon

## Pun Candidates

1. **RapSheet** — rap (rappel) + rap sheet (a personal record/history); clear journalling purpose - Too Criminal
2. **GorgeOus** — gorge + gorgeous; playful, memorable, distinctly not a guide site
3. **KnotBook** — knot (rope) + notebook; personal record feel
4. **DeScent** — descent + de-scent; evokes going down + the smell of the outdoors
5. **WetInk** — wet (canyons are wet) + wet ink (a fresh written record)
6. **InDeep** — in deep (literally in a canyon, figuratively committed)
7. **GilltyPleasure** — gill (UK canyon term) + guilty pleasure; fun and self-aware
8. **DropSheet** — drop (rappel drop) + logsheet; functional pun
9. **CordKeeper** — cord (rope) + record keeper; subtle
10. **AbSailed** — abseil + set sail; past tense suits a personal history of trips
